Environmental Sustainability

The Environmental Sustainability project began in March 2023 and focuses on promoting eco-friendly practices in South African communities.

Locations

We worked with communities in Cape Town, Stellenbosch, and Johannesburg to conduct tree planting events and promote waste management programs.

Event Overview

We partnered with local environmental organizations to plant over 5,000 trees and set up recycling stations in urban and rural areas. Community members were educated on the importance of environmental conservation and sustainability practices.
