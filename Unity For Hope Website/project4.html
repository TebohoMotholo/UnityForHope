Food Drive Initiative

The Food Drive Initiative was launched in April 2023 to combat hunger in rural and urban areas of South Africa.

Locations

This initiative took place in communities across Gauteng, including Johannesburg and Pretoria, as well as the North West province. We distributed food parcels to families in need.

Event Overview

Our team, along with volunteers, distributed over 1,500 food parcels to struggling families. We also partnered with local food banks and grocery stores to ensure a steady supply of essentials. The initiative focused on alleviating food insecurity during difficult times.

Impact

Through the Food Drive Initiative, we were able to help over 1,000 families. The gratitude and relief from the communities were overwhelming, and we continue to look for ways to provide long-term food security.
