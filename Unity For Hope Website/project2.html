Health and Wellness Camps

Since February 2023, our Health and Wellness Camps have been providing free healthcare services and health education to rural communities in South Africa.

Locations

The camps were set up in the Limpopo and Mpumalanga provinces, including villages near Polokwane and Nelspruit. Our goal was to reach communities with limited access to healthcare facilities.

Event Overview

The health camps offered free medical check-ups, vaccinations, and health workshops. We worked with local clinics and healthcare professionals to treat over 1,200 patients, many of whom had not received medical care in years.

Impact

These camps helped raise awareness about preventative healthcare and provided essential medical services to communities in need. Follow-up visits are planned to ensure continued support for these areas.
