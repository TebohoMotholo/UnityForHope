Education Drive

The Education Drive was launched in January 2023, aimed at improving access to learning materials and educational support for underprivileged communities in South Africa.

Locations

This initiative was carried out in various schools across Gauteng, KwaZulu-Natal, and the Eastern Cape. We worked with local schools in Soweto, Durban, and Mthatha to provide essential learning tools, including textbooks, stationery, and laptops.

Event Overview

The Education Drive involved setting up community workshops where students and teachers received educational resources and support. We partnered with local educational institutions and volunteers to reach over 2,500 students. The event was well-received, with a significant improvement in student engagement and learning outcomes.

Impact

The Education Drive has helped bridge the gap between under-resourced schools and those with better access to educational tools. The feedback from teachers and students has been overwhelmingly positive, and we plan to continue supporting these schools in the coming years.
